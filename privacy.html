Simple Promise: Your health data never leaves your device. We don't collect, store, transmit, or have any access to your personal health information.

Information We Access

HealthKit Exporter requests permission to read the following health data types from your device:

  • Step Count: Daily step counts from your iPhone or connected devices
  • Heart Rate: Heart rate measurements for daily averages
  • Body Weight: Weight measurements for tracking
  • Sleep Analysis: Sleep duration data

We only access data from the last 7 days for the free version of the app.

How We Use Your Data

Your health data is used exclusively for:

  • Local Processing: Converting your health data into CSV and JSON formats
  • Display: Showing you a preview of your data within the app
  • Export: Creating files that you can share using your device's built-in sharing options

Data Storage and Security

Local Processing Only: All data processing happens entirely on your device using Apple's secure HealthKit framework.

No Cloud Storage: We do not maintain any servers or cloud storage for health data.

Temporary Files: Export files are created temporarily in your device's local storage and are only accessible to you.

Apple's Security: We rely on Apple's HealthKit security protocols, which include encrypted storage and user permission controls.

Data Sharing and Export

When you export your health data:

  • Files are created locally on your device
  • You control where these files are shared (email, cloud storage, etc.)
  • We have no access to or control over where you send your exported data
  • You are responsible for the security of exported files after they leave the app

Permissions and Access

User Control: You have complete control over which health data types the app can access.

Permission Management: You can revoke access at any time through your iPhone's Health app settings.

Minimal Access: We only request access to data types that are essential for the app's core functionality.

Information We Do Collect

The app may collect minimal, non-health related information:

  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability (if you opt-in)
  • App Usage: Basic app analytics through Apple's standard frameworks (no personal data)

This information cannot be linked to your identity or health data.

Third-Party Services

HealthKit Exporter does not integrate with any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms that would have access to your health data.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is designed for general health data export and should only be used by the device owner or with appropriate parental supervision.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our app or legal requirements. We will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify users of significant changes through app updates
  • Maintain the same core principle: your health data stays private and local

Your Rights

Under various privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: View what health data the app can access (available in Health app settings)
  • Control: Enable or disable access to specific data types
  • Delete: Remove the app's access by uninstalling or changing Health app permissions
  • Transparency: Understand exactly how your data is used (outlined in this policy)
